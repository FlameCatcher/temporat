RedShift Aerospace

RedShift Aero is an initiative led by students from the Faculty of Physics, Babeș-Bolyai University in Cluj-Napoca, providing a space dedicated to academic excellence and engineering development. Our team transforms a passion for science into tangible projects, participating in international scientific competitions and developing innovative technologies such as CanSat platforms and experimental space vehicles. Through these experiences, we prepare future engineers to push the boundaries of knowledge and contribute actively to space exploration.

Message from the Founders

RedShift Aerospace was born from a shared dream: to create a space where curiosity, ambition, and technical skill could come together to achieve something greater than ourselves. We believe that true progress is made when people are empowered to experiment, to fail, and to try again—always reaching for new heights. Our journey has been one of learning, resilience, and teamwork, and we are proud to see our vision take shape through the dedication of every member. We invite you to join us, to challenge boundaries, and to help shape the future of aerospace together.
— The Founders
