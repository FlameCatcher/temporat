RedShift Aerospace Team Regulations

  1. Full Engagement
    Every member actively contributes to projects, meetings, and tests.
    Assigned responsibilities must be completed on time and with attention to detail.
  2. Open and Respectful Communication
    Discussions are conducted with respect and transparency.
    Criticism is constructive, and all ideas are heard and valued.
  3. Rigorous Planning and Organization
    Deadlines are respected, and every task is clearly documented.
    Meetings must have a clear agenda and defined objectives.
  4. Collaboration and Mutual Support
    Team members work together and provide help when challenges arise.
    Tasks are distributed according to skills and interests.
  5. Integrity and Academic Responsibility
    Team materials and ideas remain confidential until official release.
    Ethical standards and research integrity are strictly observed.
  6. Safety and Compliance with Technical Protocols
    Equipment and tests are used according to safety protocols.
    Any incidents or risks are reported immediately to the coordinator.
  7. Continuous Improvement
    The team is open to feedback and innovation.
    Progress and workflows are regularly reviewed for optimization.
